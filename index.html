Policy & Information

Here's exactly how we work, what we promise, and what we don't.

What we promise — and what we don't

We can't promise that your views or your audience will grow. We rarely pay to boost anything — we simply post your work. Whether people watch is entirely their choice.

We never take credit for what you make. And we won't use your image — which is why we ask that you don't send us photos of yourself unless we've asked for them first.

🎵 If you send a song

We'll create a lyric video for it, publish it on YouTube, and share it across our social media. (If what you send is already a lyric video, follow the "If you send a video" guidance instead.)

🎬 If you send a video

Make sure it's final and ready to publish — we won't edit or change your video in any way. We'll post it on YouTube and then share it on our social media.

📄 If you send anything else

We only review video and audio. Anything that isn't a video or an audio file won't be reviewed.

🎨 Writers, painters & craft artists

We'd love to help promote your work — but since we don't post photos, you'll need to send us a video of your art.

A few more things to know

  • The Code for Artists does not promote products of any kind — no T-shirts, mugs, or merchandise.
  • We usually don't reply on weekends.
  • Publishing can take anywhere from 2 days to 2 weeks.
